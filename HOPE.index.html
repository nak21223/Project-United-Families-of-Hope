About The Unbroken Bond

Founded by a certified Family Peer Advocate, The Unbroken Bond is dedicated to helping families overcome the challenges that lead to separation and brokenness. We believe that every family holds the potential for healing, and our mission is to provide the compassionate guidance and practical tools needed to rebuild trust, improve communication, and restore bonds.

Meet Your Coach

As the Founder & Family Intervention Coach, I bring a unique blend of personal lived experience and professional training to every family I work with. My journey has taught me the power of hope and the importance of having a guide to navigate difficult times. I am committed to empowering you with the strategies to find your way back to each other.

My provisional certification as a Family Peer Advocate in NYS provides a strong foundation, ensuring that our work together is grounded in empathy, best practices, and a deep understanding of the family system.
