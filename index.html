About The United Families of Hope

Our Mission

The United Families of Hope Association is dedicated to strengthening families by providing peer-to-peer support, encouragement, and a safe space to share experiences. We believe in the power of lived experience to inspire hope and foster resilience. Our mission is to empower families through collaborative growth, grounded in the core values of hope, support, community, and empowerment. As a volunteer Peer Specialist, I use my personal journey to connect with and guide others, offering a unique and empathetic perspective.

Family Support Group

Join a safe and confidential space to connect with other families, share your experiences, and learn from one another. Our support groups are facilitated by trained volunteer Peer Specialists who use their lived experiences to create an environment of understanding and mutual empowerment.

Peer Coaching Circles

Our coaching circles provide a collaborative setting for families to set goals, build new skills, and navigate challenges with the guidance of a Peer Specialist.

Educational Workshops

We offer workshops on a range of topics relevant to families, providing practical tools and information to help you manage everyday challenges and foster a positive home environment.

Resource Sharing & Community Referrals

We maintain a curated list of valuable community resources and can connect you with local organizations that offer services such as therapy, housing assistance, and more.
